The Ethereal Lindsey Sterling

Bio

Lindsey Sterling is an American violinist, songwriter, and dancer. She presents choreographed violin performances, in live and music videos found on her official YouTube channel, which she created in 2007. Sterling performs a variety of music styles, from classical to pop and rock to electronic dance music.
